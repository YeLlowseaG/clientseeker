# Privacy Policy for ClientSeeker

**Effective Date:** January 3, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to ClientSeeker, a professional customer development tool that helps sales teams and businesses quickly find target customer contact information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
- **What We Collect**: Name, email address, company information, and payment details
- **Purpose**: Account management, service provision, and billing

### 2. Search Data
- **What We Collect**: Search queries, selected regions, and search preferences
- **Purpose**: To provide search results and improve service quality
- **Retention**: Search history is retained for 30 days for service optimization

### 3. Usage Analytics
- **What We Collect**: Service usage patterns, feature utilization, and performance metrics
- **Purpose**: Service improvement and user experience optimization
- **Data Processing**: Anonymized and aggregated for analysis

### 4. Technical Information
- **What We Collect**: IP address, browser type, device information, and access logs
- **Purpose**: Security, fraud prevention, and service optimization

## How We Collect Business Contact Information

### Data Sources
We obtain business contact information through:
- **Public Records**: Publicly available business directories and registrations
- **Third-Party APIs**: Licensed data from Gaode Maps, Baidu Maps, and Google Maps
- **Web Scraping**: Automated collection from publicly accessible websites
- **Business Listings**: Official business directory services

### Data Processing Principles
- We only collect publicly available business information
- No unauthorized access to private databases
- Compliance with robots.txt and website terms of service
- Regular data validation and cleanup processes

## How We Use Your Information

### Service Provision
- Providing customer search and contact discovery services
- Processing and fulfilling search requests
- Generating and delivering search results and exports

### Service Improvement
- Analyzing usage patterns to enhance functionality
- Developing new features based on user needs
- Optimizing search algorithms and data quality

### Communication
- Sending service-related notifications
- Providing customer support
- Sharing important updates and changes

### Legal Compliance
- Complying with applicable laws and regulations
- Responding to legal requests and court orders
- Protecting our rights and preventing fraud

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information in these circumstances:

### Service Providers
- Third-party vendors who assist in service delivery
- Payment processors for billing purposes
- Cloud hosting providers for data storage
- All bound by strict confidentiality agreements

### Legal Requirements
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To prevent fraud or security threats
- In connection with business transfers or mergers

### User Consent
- When you explicitly authorize information sharing
- For specific purposes with your clear consent

## Data Security and Protection

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access with strong authentication
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Data Backup**: Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures

### Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while account is active plus 2 years
- Search data: 30 days for optimization, then anonymized
- Payment data: As required by financial regulations
- Support communications: 3 years for quality assurance

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- **Account Access**: View and update your account information
- **Data Export**: Download your search history and account data
- **Search History**: Delete individual searches or clear all history
- **Account Deletion**: Request complete account and data removal

### Communication Preferences
- **Email Settings**: Opt out of marketing communications
- **Notification Control**: Manage service-related notifications
- **Support Preferences**: Choose preferred communication channels

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of incorrect data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to restrict processing

## International Data Transfers

If you access ClientSeeker from outside our primary jurisdiction, your information may be transferred, stored, and processed in countries where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## Children's Privacy

ClientSeeker is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.
